ILV is an ERC-20 token on Ethereum blockchain. It is the primary token for the Illuvium ecosystem and is used for governance. ILV is used to vote in members of the Illuvium DAO Council, which make important governing decisions.

It is also interesting for investors, as it can also be staked for Revenue Distribution (RevDis) rewards. 100% of the revenue earned by the Illuvium DAO is returned to staked ILV token holders in the form of RevDis rewards. This includes all income earned by IlluviDex marketplace fees, player purchases of Fuel for Travel, Shards, Crafting, as well as all future Illuvium games, movies and products.

Note: ILV token is not used for any in-game purchases or P2P transactions.

Fuel is the official power source of Illuvium. In Illuvium Zero, high level technological upgrades as well as the researching of biodata will cost fuel. Outside of the land game, any progression in the illuvium universe will require Fuel. Since it has such a large use-case, fuel will be coveted by everyone playing the Illuvium games.

There are three types of Fuel: Crypton, Hyperion and Solon.

Crypton Crypton is the Green colored fuel type that is used to power the obelisks in the overworld. You won't be able to travel to a different obelisk to catch T1-T5 illuvials without it!

Hyperion Hyperion is the rainbow colored fuel type that is used to cure shards in the overworld. Shards with a tiers 1 to 5 will require Hyperion to be cured. but they are worth it, because the capture rates of Illuvials will increase with higher tiered shards!

Solon Solon is the golden colored fuel type that can be used to forge and upgrade equipment!

Fuel is connected to the Illuvium Overworld and Arena economy; additionally, it will be connected to all future Illuvium games.
